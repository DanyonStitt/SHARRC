About

Enhancing humanity through innovative solutions in sports engineering, sports performance, health, and rehabilitation to active lifestyles.
At SHARRC, we believe that technological innovation can play a crucial role in improving sports access, performance, equipment, and rehabilitation outcomes. We are committed to using innovative approaches to improve health outcomes and assist with Sport NZ's vision of "getting Every Body Active in Aotearoa New Zealand." Our team is dedicated to making a positive impact on the health of our nation, and we invite you to join us in promoting a healthy and active lifestyle.
